Steps to Bake a Cake

Baking a cake is a fun and rewarding process. Follow these simple steps to create a delicious homemade cake.

Ingredients

Before starting, gather the following ingredients: flour, sugar, eggs, butter, baking powder, vanilla extract, and milk.

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Grease and flour your cake pans to prevent sticking.
  3. In a large bowl, cream together the butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  4. Add the eggs one at a time, beating well with each addition.
  5. In a separate bowl, sift together the flour and baking powder.
  6. Gradually add the flour mixture to the butter mixture, alternating with the milk.
  7. Stir in the vanilla extract.
  8. Pour the batter evenly into the prepared cake pans.
  9. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  10. Remove the cakes from the oven and let them cool in the pans for 10 minutes.
  11. Turn the cakes out onto a wire rack to cool completely before frosting.
