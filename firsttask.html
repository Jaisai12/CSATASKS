Main Topic (h1)

This is the main title of the document. Typically, there is only one <h1> on a page, and it's the most important heading.

Section Heading (h2)

This is a section heading under the main topic. It is less important than <h1>, but still a major section title.

Subsection Heading (h3)

This is a subsection under the <h2> heading. It’s used to break down content further.

Detail Heading (h4)

This is a heading under <h3> for further details. It is used to organize smaller sections.

Minor Detail Heading (h5)

This is a minor section under <h4>. It’s typically used for specific details within a larger topic.

Least Important Heading (h6)

This is the least important heading in the hierarchy. It is rarely used but can be useful for the smallest subdivisions of content.
